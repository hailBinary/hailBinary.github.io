Projects I made in college,

  1. Visioalgo: A path finding and sorting visualiser
    Me and my friends made a pathfinding, djikstra and a*, and sorting, bubble and merge sort, algorithm visulaiser for our college project.

  2. Self-DrivingCar Simulation
    Made this one in pure javascript. A self driving car simulation operates on a feed forward neural net. The neural net saves the car that goes the farthest among many cars with slight variations and then uses the winning car to build the next generation of cars and so on. Was fun to build.

  3. GrabBag
    A clickable quiz game I made using p5.js for my college quiz game. There are 4 * 4 boxes and only 7 of them are correct. Click on the correct ones to win points.

  4. RL enemy bots in an FPS shooter game
    My final thesis for my undergraduate where we trained enemy bots using RL in unity for an fps shooter games. The bots showed intelligent behavior better than traditional bots.
